Toothpaste: Cherry

The toothpaste stains your teeth red! You try to wash off the red, but it won't come out. Your brother, hearing you make a commotion about your teeth, barges into the bathroom to find out what's going on. He sees your dillema and hands you a white tank top. He whispers a funny prank idea to you. You grab your stuff and head into your car. As you drive to school you think about the prank. Will you do the prank, or will you not do the prank?
